completed orders\n- **Avg Daily Sales**: Average revenue per day\n- **Peak Sales Day**: Day with highest revenue\n- **Sales Trend**: Revenue trend over period\n\n### Customer Metrics\n- **Total Customers**: Unique customer count\n- **New Customers**: First-time purchasers\n- **Returning Customers**: Repeat purchasers\n- **Churn Rate**: % of customers who stopped purchasing\n- **Retention Rate**: % of customers who continue purchasing\n- **Customer LTV**: Average lifetime value\n\n### Order Metrics\n- **Total Orders**: Number of completed orders\n- **Avg Order Value**: Average order amount\n- **Median Order Value**: Middle value of orders\n- **Order Frequency**: Average orders per customer\n- **Repeat Order Rate**: % of repeat customers\n\n### Conversion Metrics\n- **Conversion Rate**: % of visitors who purchase\n- **Cart Abandonment**: % of carts not completed\n- **Funnel Conversion**: Stage-by-stage conversion\n\n## Troubleshooting\n\n### No Data Displayed\n- Verify
