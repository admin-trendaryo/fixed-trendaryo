Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2024

30-Day Money Back Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our products! If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. No questions asked.

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, items must meet the following criteria:

  • Returned within 30 days of delivery date
  • Unused and in the same condition as received
  • In original packaging with all tags attached
  • Proof of purchase (order number or receipt)

How to Request a Return

Step 1: Contact our support team at returns@trendaryo.com with your order number and reason for return.

Step 2: We'll send you a prepaid return shipping label within 24 hours.

Step 3: Pack the item securely in its original packaging and attach the label.

Step 4: Drop off at any authorized shipping location.

Step 5: Receive your refund within 5-7 business days after we receive your return.

Refund Processing

Once we receive your return, our team will inspect it within 2 business days. If approved, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for purchase. You'll receive an email confirmation once the refund is processed.

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? We're happy to exchange your item! Follow the same return process and mention you'd like an exchange. We'll ship your replacement item as soon as we receive your return.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned for hygiene and safety reasons:

  • Personalized or custom-made products
  • Intimate apparel and swimwear
  • Beauty and personal care items (opened)
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance"
  • Gift cards and downloadable products

Damaged or Defective Items

Received a damaged or defective item? We sincerely apologize! Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the issue. We'll send a replacement immediately at no cost or issue a full refund including shipping charges.

Return Shipping Costs

Return shipping is FREE for US customers - we provide a prepaid label. International customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or we made an error.
