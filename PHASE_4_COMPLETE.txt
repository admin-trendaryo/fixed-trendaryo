5. MANAGE SUBSCRIPTIONS:
   const result = await window.EmailManager.subscribeToEmails(email, preferences);

================================================================================
🐛 TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

ISSUE: "Email not sent"
SOLUTION: Check email address, verify subscription, check preferences, review logs

ISSUE: "Unsubscribe not working"
SOLUTION: Verify email address, check subscription status, clear cache, try again

ISSUE: "Preferences not saving"
SOLUTION: Verify authentication, check email address, verify format, check logs

ISSUE: "Email history empty"
SOLUTION: Check if emails were sent, verify date range, check status, review logs

ISSUE: "Email template not rendering"
SOLUTION: Check template format, verify data, check email client, review logs
