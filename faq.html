FAQ

How do I track my order?
+
You can track your order by visiting our Track Order page and entering your order number. You'll receive real-time updates on your shipment status.
What is your return policy?
+
We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused items in original packaging. Simply contact our support team to initiate a return.
How long does shipping take?
+
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available for 2-3 business days delivery. Free shipping on orders over $50.
Is my payment information secure?
+
Yes! We use SSL encryption and secure payment gateways to protect your data. All transactions are PCI-compliant.
How do I contact support?
+
You can reach our 24/7 support team via email at support@trendaryo.com, through our contact form, or by calling +1 (555) 123-4567.
