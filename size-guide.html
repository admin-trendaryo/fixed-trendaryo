Clothing Size Guide

📏 How to Measure

Chest/Bust

  • Measure around the fullest part
  • Keep tape horizontal
  • Wear light clothing

Waist

  • Find natural waistline
  • Measure around narrowest part
  • Keep tape snug but comfortable

Hips

  • Stand with feet together
  • Measure around fullest part
  • Keep tape horizontal
💡 Fit Tip: If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more relaxed fit or sizing down for a fitted look.
